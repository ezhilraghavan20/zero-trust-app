# Module 4 – Behavior Analytics

Zero Trust behavior analytics module.

## Goals
- Establish a behavioral baseline per identity
- Detect anomalous behavior at request time
- Generate normalized behavior risk signals
- Remain stateless during access evaluation

## Rules
- Past behavior does NOT guarantee future trust
- Behavior is evaluated continuously and contextually
- Behavior analytics produces risk signals, not access decisions
- DO NOT authenticate users
- DO NOT evaluate device posture
- DO NOT analyze location/network context
- DO NOT calculate final trust score
- DO NOT allow or deny access
